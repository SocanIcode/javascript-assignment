1. Introduction

At Rainday, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect the information you provide when using our website, products, or services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide you with the best service and experience:

  • Usage Data: We may collect data on how you interact with our website, such as IP addresses, browser types, pages viewed, and time spent on the site.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for various purposes, including:

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. These providers are obligated to protect your data and only use it for the specific services they are hired to perform.

6. Your Rights
